Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ClickKloudy.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the required information.

How do I set up my profile?

Navigate to the Settings page, and under "Profile Settings," you can update your details and upload a profile picture.

Social Media Management

How do I link my social media accounts?

Go to the Settings Page, select "Account Management," and click "Add Account." Follow the prompts to authenticate.

Can I manage accounts from different platforms?

Yes, ClickKloudy supports multiple social media platforms, allowing you to manage accounts from different services in one place.

Can I schedule posts?

You can use the scheduling feature on the dashboard to set up posts for a specific date and time. ClickKloudy will handle the rest and publish your post automatically.

What is the maximum number of accounts I can manage?

The maximum number of accounts you can manage depends on your subscription plan. For free users, the limit is 10 accounts. Paid plans allow up to 1,000 accounts.

Pricing and Plans

What does the free plan include?

The free plan includes basic features such as account linking and post scheduling, with a limit of 10 linked accounts.

How do I upgrade to a premium plan?

Visit the Subscription Management section in Settings to view and select available plans.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to the Settings Page, select "Subscription Management," and click "Cancel Subscription." Follow the confirmation steps.

Technical Issues

What should I do if I can't log in?

Ensure your credentials are correct. If the issue persists, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

How do I report a bug?

Contact support via the "Help" section in your dashboard and provide a detailed description of the issue.
