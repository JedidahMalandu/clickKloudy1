ClickKloudy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Information Collection

When you use ClickKloudy's social media management platform, we collect:

  • Social media account credentials and access tokens
  • Account information (name, email, business details)
  • Content posted through our platform
  • Usage analytics and engagement metrics
  • Payment and billing information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Connect and manage your social media accounts
  • Schedule and post content across platforms
  • Generate analytics and performance reports
  • Process payments and maintain your subscription
  • Improve our platform features and user experience

3. Social Media Platform Integration

ClickKloudy integrates with multiple social media platforms. When you connect your accounts:

  • We access only the permissions you explicitly grant
  • We follow each platform's terms of service and API guidelines
  • You can revoke access at any time
  • We maintain secure handling of access tokens

4. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure data centers and backup systems
  • Strict access controls and authentication

5. Your Rights

As a ClickKloudy user, you can:

  • Access and export your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request information about your data usage

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect:

  • New platform features and capabilities
  • Changes in data protection regulations
  • Improved security practices
  • User feedback and concerns
